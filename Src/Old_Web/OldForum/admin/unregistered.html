License Key Registration
Purchasing a license key will remove all banners and give your users access to the Rich Text Editor. Follow the directions below to obtain a license key.
 
Step 1:
Click here to visit SolarianLeague.com and purchase a license key.
 
Step 2:
After purchasing Toast, send an email to license@SolarianLeague.com with the information in the box below. You may copy the info from the box below and paste it into your email if you like. After verifing your payment, you will be sent a license key within 24 hours (usually within 1-2 hours).


 
Step 3:
When you receive the email, follow the directions to install your license key. After successfully installing it, you will no longer see this screen; you will be shown licensing information instead.
